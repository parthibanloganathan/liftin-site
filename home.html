FAQ

What is this?

It's a calculator to determine how much you should lift while staying safe.

What is RPE?

RPE is rate of perceived exertion. It's a scale that ranges from 6.5 to 10 for lifting where 6.5 is almost a warm up and you could do 3-4 more reps. 10 is failure on a set where you absolutely can't do another rep.

How do I use Liftin?

Let's say you just completed a set of 6 reps with 150 lbs on the bar and could've done 2 more reps. That's an RPE 8. Liftin helps you answer questions such as what's the max weight you could safely lift for a 3 reps at RPE 9?
